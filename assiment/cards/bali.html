Bali, Indonesia

Experience tropical beaches, lush forests, and vibrant culture.

Highlights:
  • Uluwatu Temple
  • Ubud Monkey Forest
  • Rice Terraces
  • Seminyak & Kuta Beaches
Culture & Cuisine:

Bali is famous for its temples, traditional dances, and delicious Indonesian food. Try nasi goreng and satay.

Best Time to Visit:

April to October for dry weather and outdoor activities.
