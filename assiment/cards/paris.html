Paris, France

The city of lights, romance, and world-famous landmarks.

Highlights:
  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Seine River
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur
Culture & Cuisine:

Paris is renowned for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Enjoy French pastries, wine, and world-class shopping.

Best Time to Visit:

Spring and autumn for mild weather and fewer crowds.
